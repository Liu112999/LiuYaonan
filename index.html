Research研究方向

Three connected threads三条相互交织的主线

My work combines environmental policy evaluation with urban economic analysis to find where regulation can be both efficient and equitable.我的研究将环境政策评估与城市经济分析相结合,探寻监管在何处既能提升效率、又能促进公平。

Market-Based Environmental Regulation市场化环境监管与能源转型

How incentive-based rules shape firms' emissions and the shift to clean energy — using NASA satellite data to evaluate China's carbon markets.基于激励的监管如何影响企业排放与清洁能源转型——借助 NASA 卫星数据评估中国碳市场试点。

Housing Affordability & Rent Regulation住房可负担性与租金管制

Who really benefits from rent stabilization across income, race, immigration status, and age — drawing on two decades of New York City data.在收入、种族、移民身份与年龄维度上,究竟谁从租金稳定政策中获益——基于纽约市近二十年的数据。

Climate, Adaptation & the City气候变化、适应与城市

How climate shocks interact with historical housing discrimination, such as redlining, to shape urban adaptation and sustainability.气候冲击如何与历史住房歧视(如"红线政策")交互,影响城市的适应与可持续发展。
